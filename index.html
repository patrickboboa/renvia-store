Sound familiar?

If you sit a lot — or stand all day — your legs pay the price

By mid-afternoon your legs feel like lead. Your ankles puff up. Your feet go cold and tingly at night. You prop them up, you wear the socks, you keep moving — but the heavy, achy feeling always comes back.

Here's the part most people never hear: as we get older — and especially when we sit for long stretches — the muscles in our lower legs stop squeezing as often. Those muscles are the pump that pushes blood back up from your feet. When the pump slows down, blood pools, and that's the heavy, swollen, tired feeling you know too well.

The mechanism

Renvia gently restarts your leg's natural pump
